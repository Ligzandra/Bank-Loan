MARY-ALEX LOAN CRITERIA


  1. 1. At the point of request, loan applicant's current account balance should not excede intending loan request.

  2. 2. Loan Applicants must have a good 6months credit history.

  3. 3. Loan Applicants must have deposited into their accounts within 30days.

  4. 4. Loan Applicant must not be in receipt of or have received any loan from this bank within the last 6 months.

  5. 5. Loan Applicant is able to repay his loan within the space of 6 months.

  6. 6. It is advisable for loan applicant to preferably have a current account with the bank.
